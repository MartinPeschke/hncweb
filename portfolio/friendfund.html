Community Funding

Friendfund is powered by a flexible, purpose built group payment engine that easily accommodates single as well as group transactions, configurable staggered discount models, funding over-subscription and debt workflows for refused payments.

The friendfund group payment engine can be integrated into any merchant shop through its white labelling portal. Integration can be as simple as copy & pasting the 'friendfund it' button, a javascript snippet not unlike the Facebook Like button. This button leads customers through a custom branded group payment system powered by friendfund.

The Funding Community

Friendfund and all white labels contain the full set of social media integrations, sharing widgets and behavioural tracking tools.
